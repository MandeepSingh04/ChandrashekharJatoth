About Me

I'm Dr. Chandrashekhar Jatoth

Assistant Professor (GR-II) NIT Raipur (Department of Information Technology)

I am an Assistant Professor (GR-II) in Departmentof Information Technology of NIT Raipur, holding a Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence. With a Bachelor's and Master's in Engineering from the University of Hyderabad, I bring a strong educational background to my work. My research interests include Cloud/Fog Computing, Energy Informatics, Artificial Intelligence, and Computational Intelligence Techniques. I am passionate about exploring the intersection between these fields and incorporating my findings into my research and teaching activities. As an Assistant Professor, I am dedicated to sharing my diverse perspectives on these topics with my students.
